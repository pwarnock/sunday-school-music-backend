# Daily Prayer

*"Rejoice always, pray continually, give thanks in all circumstances; for this is God's will for you in Christ Jesus."* - 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18

## Morning Prayer

Heavenly Father, as I begin this new day, I thank You for Your faithfulness and love. Guide my steps, guard my heart, and help me to walk in Your ways. Give me wisdom in my work, patience in my trials, and compassion for those around me. May everything I do today bring glory to Your name. In Jesus' name, Amen.

## Evening Prayer

Lord, as this day comes to a close, I thank You for Your presence with me. Forgive me where I have fallen short of Your glory. Help me to rest in Your peace and wake tomorrow with renewed strength to serve You. Bless my loved ones and keep us all in Your care. In Christ's name, Amen.
